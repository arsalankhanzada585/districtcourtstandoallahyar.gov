About the Court

History, jurisdiction, and the judicial structure of the District & Sessions Court, Tando Allahyar.

Overview

A district court serving the people of Tando Allahyar

The District & Sessions Court, Tando Allahyar is the principal court of original civil and criminal jurisdiction for the district, functioning under the administrative control of the Honourable High Court of Sindh. It supervises the subordinate judiciary of the district, including the courts of Civil Judges, Judicial Magistrates, and specialized tribunals.

The District Judge, as head of the district judiciary, hears civil appeals and revisions, while the Sessions Judge exercises criminal jurisdiction including trials of serious offences and criminal appeals from the Magistracy.

The court complex also houses the Family Court, Consumer Court, and the Judicial Magistrate courts, bringing most judicial forums for the district under a single premises for the convenience of litigants and lawyers.

Jurisdiction

What this court hears

  • S
    Sessions / Criminal
    Trials of sessions-triable offences and criminal appeals from Magistrate courts
  • C
    Civil
    Civil suits, appeals, and revisions arising within the district
  • F
    Family
    Dissolution of marriage, maintenance, custody, and guardianship matters
  • A
    Administrative
    Supervision of subordinate courts and judicial magistracy in the district
